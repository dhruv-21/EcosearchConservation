HOPE-Turtle born with an exposed Heart
Hope is an Albino turtle who was born with an exposed heart..
She is very rare and her owner Michael Aquilina (known as AquaMike23 on Instagram )says that she is the only turtle of her kind..She has a hole in her shell righ over the heart
When Michael found her he decided to give her some extra time..
He remade one of his turtle tanks for hope and used special equippments like sponge filter and had to set the temperature of the water accordingly..
He covered the tank with algae and moss balls to avoid any accident
In the very beggining when he brought hope in November 2018, It's size was very small (Almost the size of a coin)...
But hopefully now Hope is getting bigger day by day ..
